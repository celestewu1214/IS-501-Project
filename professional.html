My Professional Focus

Public Administration and Community Impact

My Career Goal

My goal is to work in local government. I want to help improve public programs and services. I am interested in program evaluation and policy analysis. I want to support better decisions and stronger communities.

My Experience

I am studying public administration at BYU. I have learned about policy, research, and data analysis. I also worked on a project about the Kids Cafe program. In this project, we discussed interviews and program evaluation.

My Contributions

I want to help local governments make better decisions. I believe public programs should be effective and fair. I want to use data to improve services for students, families, and communities.

Why This Field Matters

Public administration is important because it affects daily life. Good public service can improve education, safety, and community well-being. I want to be part of work that creates positive change.
